Become A Model

Lets try to answer your questions here!

How do i begin?

Make a deposit of 5000 naira here.  fill the form at link, you will recieve an email from us for a successful completion of form as well as another email stating your audition 

The purpose of the 5000 naira isnt for auditions! it is for server and database logistics, security and company specific processes. we absorb about 90% of applicants by creating opportunities that benefit financially. if you are unsure, please do not make this commitment.

What kinds of jobs will i do?

Depending on your preferences, you will be required to act movies, model, dance, participate in contests, tours and pageants.  we will approach exclusive designers and photographers and setup photo and video shoots for you. you will be required to travel to locations on behalf of the company and represent.

Why should i choose Nutty Models?

We believe in a digital future, one where your progress in life can be enhanced via the use of technology, we are the future of modelling across Africa.

What else should i know?

Please only submit NATURAL photographs,  we wish to see you in your most natural state.

Digitally enhanced photographs are NOT to be submitted.

All photographs are to be sent in colour.

Underwear shots will be required, if you don't feel comfortable please do not apply.

Please do not wear make-up, as we must see your true skin complexion.

Hair must be naturally styled, with no visible hair products and it must not obstruct your face and bone structure in the head-shot.

Mid-length and full-length shots should be taken whilst fully clothed.

The categories for selection will be head, bust and underwear shots.

Stand up straight in the shots and try to look as natural as possible.
